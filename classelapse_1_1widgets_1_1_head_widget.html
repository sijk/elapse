Detailed Description

The HeadWidget class displays a 3D head in a configurable orientation.

The widget uses intrinsic rotations measured in radians. That is, the rotations are taken about axes that are fixed to the head, not about the world's axes. The x axis points out the right ear, the y axis out the nose, and the z axis out the top of the head.
